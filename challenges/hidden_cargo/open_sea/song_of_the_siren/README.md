# Song of the Siren

A merchant captain bound for Saltmark transmitted a recording — a sea shanty, four bars of melody, hummed through a tin telegraph at midnight — that no one in his crew claimed to recognize. He swore he hadn't sung it. We pulled the audio off the wire (`siren.wav`) and we also intercepted the chart he'd been studying that night (`chart.png`).

There's something hidden in the song. It will tell you where to look on the chart.

## Files
- `siren.wav` — the intercepted shanty
- `chart.png` — the navigation chart from the captain's table
