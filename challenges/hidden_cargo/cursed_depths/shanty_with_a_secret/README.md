# Shanty With a Secret

The *Reefborn Five* recorded an old sea shanty for one of their dockside concerts last spring — three minutes of accordion, fiddle, and four-part harmony. The MP3 they pressed onto coin-sized commemorative badges has been circulating the harbor ever since, and one copy made it onto our desk along with the printed lyrics broadside the band handed out at the show.

We have reason to believe the shanty's MP3 carries a hidden message embedded in its bit reservoir — the kind of stego that survives normal listening but yields to anyone who knows the passphrase. And we have reason to believe the printed lyrics broadside, in turn, hides that very passphrase in its typography. Neither half decodes the other alone.

Recover both halves. Combine. The Treasure is what falls out.
