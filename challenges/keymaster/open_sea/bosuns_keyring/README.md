# The Bosun's Keyring

The bosun of the *Crowsfoot Trading Company*, one Hieronymus Pegg, was famous through three ports for his appalling password discipline: every chest he locked, every archive he sealed, used a password of the form `<shipname><year><punctuation>`. We have a list of 800 ship names known to have docked at Crowsfoot's harbor between 1790 and 1810, and Pegg always picked one of those ships, one of those years, and one of `!`, `?`, or `.`.

He's left us a 7-zip archive. The Treasure is inside.

## Files
- `treasure.7z` — the password-protected archive
- `shipnames.txt` — 800 ship names
