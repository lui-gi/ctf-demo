# The Rusty Padlock

The strongbox washed up half-eaten by saltworms, its iron padlock crusted shut. The lock itself isn't what stops you — Old Bert the locksmith broke it open in twenty seconds. What stops you is the slip of parchment Bert found inside, with a single line scrawled across it:

> *MD5 challenge — bosun's word.*

> `8d3367c5e34c8eb9c41ac3f1d0e07afa`

The scuttlebutt around the wharf is that the bosun who locked this thing always picked his passwords from the same source: the produce stand by the dockmaster's office. Always a fruit. Always lowercase, no decorations.

Crack the hash. The password is the Treasure flavor.
