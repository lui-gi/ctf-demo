# Whispers — EXIF of the Drowned

## Whisper 1 (-10%)
The picture itself doesn't matter. Look at the file's metadata, not its pixels.

## Whisper 2 (-20% cumulative)
JPEG files carry EXIF tags. `exiftool wreck.jpg` will dump every tag the file has. The Treasure is written into one of the comment-style tags, not the obvious ones like `ImageDescription`.
