# The First Mate's Burner Account

We've been tracking a freelance smuggler who calls herself **Salty_Seraphina** for three months now. She runs a public crew profile on the *Albatross.social* network — all bravado, recipes for sea-biscuit, complaints about port duties, the usual.

But word in the rigging is that Seraphina runs a second account. A burner. The kind of account where she actually says useful things — names of contacts, drop coordinates, and yes, the password to the safehouse.

Her main profile is public. Find it, follow her tracks to the burner, and read whatever she's been careless enough to leave on the alt's bio.
