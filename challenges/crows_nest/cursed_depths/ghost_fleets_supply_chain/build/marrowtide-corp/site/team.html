The team

Three permanent staff. We hire deliberately and keep things small.

Sela Marrowfen

Chief Technology Officer & Co-founder

Sela spent fifteen years as a shipbuilder before turning to insurance, and she still spends two weeks a year at the yards. She designed Marrowtide's risk-modelling pipeline and gives the keynote each year at the (fictional) Cargo Underwriters Forum on the Crescent.

Kaspar Threnody

Head of Operations & Co-founder

Kaspar ran the dockyard at Tideward Quarter for twelve years and knows every harbormaster from Inner Crescent to the Outer Reefs. Off the clock he founded the (fictional) Free Tide Initiative, a non-profit pairing retired captains with new underwriters.
