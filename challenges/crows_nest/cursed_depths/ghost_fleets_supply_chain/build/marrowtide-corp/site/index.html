Insurance, underwriting, and routing for the Crescent grain trade.

For six seasons, Marrowtide has quietly become the partner of choice for shipping syndicates moving cargo through difficult waters. We write the manifests so the captains can sleep.

What we do

Marrowtide Logistics provides three core services to mid-tier grain shippers:

  • Cargo manifest validation — our internal tooling cross-checks every bill of lading before underwriting.
  • Route risk modelling — we maintain proprietary tide and weather models for the Crescent.
  • Claims arbitration — when a claim is filed, we resolve it within 14 tides.
New for this season: all underwriting now uses our updated cargo manifest validator (v2). See the engineering blog post from our senior engineering team.
