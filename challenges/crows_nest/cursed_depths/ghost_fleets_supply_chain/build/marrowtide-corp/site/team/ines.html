Ines Tidemark

Senior Engineer

Ines writes most of the software at Marrowtide. Her main project is marrowtide-internal/manifests-cli — our private tooling for cargo manifest validation — which she has maintained from its first commit. She also keeps the internal package registry running and writes the engineering blog.

Tooling she ships

  • marrowtide-internal/manifests-cli — published to npm.marrowtide.example. The repository for the public components is at git.marrowtide.example/manifests-cli.
  • @marrowtide/tide-charts — charting helpers used by the underwriting dashboard.
  • @marrowtide/keel-metrics — a small metrics shim around our risk-model output.
