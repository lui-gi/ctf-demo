Kaspar Threnody

Head of Operations & Co-founder

Kaspar ran the dockyard at Tideward Quarter for twelve years before joining Sela to start Marrowtide. He is the company's main point of contact with harbourmasters, captains-of-record, and the various port authorities that handle our shipper clients' cargo.

Free Tide Initiative

Off the clock, Kaspar founded the (fictional) Free Tide Initiative, a small non-profit that pairs retired captains with new underwriters for ride-along tours. It runs out of Pier 11.
