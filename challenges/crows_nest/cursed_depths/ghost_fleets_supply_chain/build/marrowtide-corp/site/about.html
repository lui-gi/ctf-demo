About Marrowtide

Marrowtide Logistics was founded in the autumn of 2025 by a small team of shipbuilders, dockyard operations leads, and engineers who had spent decades watching the Crescent grain trade lose money on bad insurance contracts.

Our origin

The original three founders had each, in their own way, run aground on the existing market. One had built ships that were declared total losses on dubious technicalities. Another had managed dockyards where a missed manifest line could wipe out a season's margin. The third had written the software that everyone else used to file the paperwork — and watched it get misused for years.

Marrowtide is what they built when they were tired of patching other people's mistakes.

Our footprint

We are a small company by design. As of this season, we run with a permanent staff of three plus a rotating bench of contracted underwriters and captains-of-record. The three permanent staff are listed on our Team page.

Transparency

We operate publicly where we can. Our internal engineering tooling is published to our private package registry at npm.marrowtide.example; the source for the open-licensed components is mirrored to our self-hosted git at git.marrowtide.example; and every TLS certificate we have ever issued for any of our hostnames is publicly searchable at crt.marrowtide.example as part of our transparency pledge.
