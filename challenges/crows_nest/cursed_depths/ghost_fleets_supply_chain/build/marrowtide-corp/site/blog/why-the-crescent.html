Why the Crescent (and why now)

2026-03-03 · by Sela Marrowfen

The Crescent grain trade has been underwritten by the same three or four firms for the better part of a generation. None of them have updated their tide models since well before the silt shift of 2021.

That is the gap. We started Marrowtide to close it.

This is also why we are aggressively transparent about our infrastructure: every certificate we have ever issued for any of our hostnames is searchable at crt.marrowtide.example. If anyone, including us, stands up a service under our name, you can audit it.
