# The Ghost Fleet's Supply Chain

A shell company called *Marrowtide Logistics* has, in the last six months, quietly bought up the entire shipping insurance market for the Crescent's grain trade. They have a slick corporate site, three named employees with public bios, a private NPM registry hosting their internal tooling, and a public certificate-transparency log we managed to mirror.

But Marrowtide is a front. Somewhere across their public footprint — between an employee's bio, a leaked NPM package, an issued certificate, a `.well-known` file, and a static page no one's supposed to find — the real owners hid a sentence. That sentence IS the Treasure.

Every step of the trail is a logical breadcrumb. No guessing. The first step is *marrowtide.example* (provided at Voyage start); the last step is reading a sentence assembled from clues across the whole footprint.
