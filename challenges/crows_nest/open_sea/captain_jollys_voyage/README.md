# Captain Jolly's Last Voyage

Captain "Jolly" Wexler is a small-time pirate-blogger who runs a public travel diary chronicling his voyages around the lesser-known harbors of the Crescent. His most recent post — *"Sunset on a forgotten quay"* — went up three days ago, then he went silent. Crew never came home. We want to know where he was.

His blog has the post (with the picture, EXIF stripped). His parallel "tide journal" RSS feed records the high tide at his current location, with timestamps. We host an archive of historical tide tables for every charted harbor in the Crescent. Cross-reference the tide reading he posted against the historical tables.

The Treasure is the literal name of the harbor where Jolly took the photograph.
