Harbour tide archive

The Authority maintains hourly tide observations for every charted harbour in the Crescent — twelve at present. Each harbour's record is a CSV with three columns: timestamp_utc, tide_m, direction. The current rolling window is 2026-03-30T00:00:00Z through 2026-04-29T00:00:00Z (721 hourly observations per harbour).

Mariners and bloggers occasionally publish their own tide journals (e.g. /blog/jolly-wexler/tide.rss) and ask us to verify their readings. To do that you can match a (timestamp, tide, direction) triple from a journal entry against any of the CSVs below; the harbour whose record contains that exact row is the one the journal was filed from.
