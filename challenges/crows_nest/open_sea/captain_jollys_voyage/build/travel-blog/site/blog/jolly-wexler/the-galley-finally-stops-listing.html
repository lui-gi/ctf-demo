The galley finally stops listing

2026-04-21 · by Jolly Wexler

Three weeks at sea and the gimbal mount on the stove was off by enough that the pot would slide every time we crested anything bigger than a swell. I have been eating raw bread for the better part of a fortnight. Today I finally pulled the whole assembly out, found the bolt that had backed itself out half a turn, tightened it, and reseated everything. Stew for dinner.

The wind has been steady from the west for three days. Tide has been steady too — nothing dramatic, nothing worth writing home about. I'll keep posting the readings to the journal, but if you're looking for excitement, this is not the week.

Tomorrow I run east toward the next anchorage. Three days of easy reaching if the wind holds.
