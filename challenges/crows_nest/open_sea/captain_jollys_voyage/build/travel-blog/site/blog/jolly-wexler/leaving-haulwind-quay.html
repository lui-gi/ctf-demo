Leaving Haulwind Quay

2026-02-18 · by Jolly Wexler

This is the start of the spring run. I have provisioned for forty days, which is twice as long as I think the run will actually take, and twice as little as I would prefer. Forty days of salt cod, hard biscuit, and small comforts.

The wind is fair from the west, the sea is rough enough to be entertaining without being rude. Hesper is heeled over comfortably and the rail is dipping just enough to keep me honest. I'll keep to the lesser charts, as I always do, and I'll post when I make port. Tide journal as usual.

If you don't hear from me for a fortnight, that's normal. If you don't hear from me for a season, send a search party.

Onwards.
