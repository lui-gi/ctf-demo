# Whispers — The Captain's Caesar

## Whisper 1 (-10%)
You already know what every progctf flag starts with. Look for those seven letters somewhere in the parchment, scrambled by the same amount as everything else.

## Whisper 2 (-20% cumulative)
A Caesar cipher just shifts every letter by a fixed amount around the alphabet. There are only 25 possible shifts — try them all (CyberChef has a "ROT13 Brute Force" recipe), or compute the shift directly from the first letter of the flag token.
