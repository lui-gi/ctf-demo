Two Pcaps, One Boarding — player-served files
================================================
ftp.pcap   — cleartext FTP session (port 21 control + 20 data)
             Captain logs in and downloads shell_history.txt.enc.
             The encrypted blob format is OpenSSL `enc -aes-256-cbc`
             with the standard `Salted__` + 8-byte salt + ct layout.
usb.pcap   — USB-HID keyboard capture (USBPCAP link-type 249).
             Decode with Wireshark + en-US HID keymap or tshark fields.
