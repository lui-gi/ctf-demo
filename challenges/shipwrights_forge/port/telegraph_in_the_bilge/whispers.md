# Whispers — Telegraph in the Bilge

## Whisper 1 (-10%)
The pcap captures a single conversation between two hosts on a well-known port. Identify the protocol Wireshark labels it as.

## Whisper 2 (-20% cumulative)
Telnet sends every keystroke in plaintext. In Wireshark, right-click any packet from this conversation and use **Follow → TCP Stream** to see the entire exchange reassembled.
