# Telegraph in the Bilge

Two ships rendezvoused off the Salt Spire last fortnight — the *Bilgewater* and the *Long Dread* — and our spies in the rigging managed to tap their telegraph wire before they cast off again. Whatever password the Bilgewater's quartermaster tapped across to the Long Dread's boarding officer was carried in the clear, like fools shouting across a tavern.

Our spies handed us the recording: a single small pcap of the exchange. The boarding password they used IS the Treasure we want.

Open the capture, follow the conversation, and read the password right out of it.
