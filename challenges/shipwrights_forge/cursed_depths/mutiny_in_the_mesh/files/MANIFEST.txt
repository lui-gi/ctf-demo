Mutiny in the Mesh — player-served files
==========================================
buoy.elf            — ARM Cortex-M4 firmware (stripped). Reverse to recover
                      the wire protocol and the per-buoy SEED constants.
buoy_a.pcap         — UDP/8421 traffic from BUOY_A
buoy_b.pcap         — UDP/8421 traffic from BUOY_B
buoy_c.pcap         — UDP/8421 traffic from BUOY_C
buoy_d.pcap         — UDP/8421 traffic from BUOY_D
All pcaps record the harbormaster receiving sensor reports from the mesh.
One of the buoys carries traffic the rest do not. That is your starting
point.
